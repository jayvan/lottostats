Lottery Galaxy - Results, Statistics, Odds and Prizes

Introduction

Playing the lottery can be fun and exciting, but it pays to play smart. All lotteries are different, from their jackpots to their odds. Every week the jackpots and smaller prizes change based on how much of the prize pool was won, and how many tickets are expected to be sold. Here on Lottery Galaxy I've broken down each lottery and calculated how much money you can expect to win. Hopefully the information provided on this site helps you to make informed lottery decisions.
