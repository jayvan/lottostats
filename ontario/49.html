About Ontario 49

Ontario 49 is an Ontario exclusive lottery that is drawn every Wednesday and Saturday night at 10:30PM EST. A single entry costs $1. The jackpot amount and all other prizes are exactly the same each week. The jackpot and "5/6 + Bonus" prize are shared by all ticket holders. The odds of winning each prize is the same as Lotto 6/49, however tickets cost $1 and prizes are smaller.
