About Wheel of Fortune

Wheel of Fortune Lotto is one of Ontario's instant win games, which give you a chance to win right away as well as a ticket to a lottery that night. For each $2 play you will see the prize wheel spin on the lottery terminal. On the prize wheel you can win up to $10,000 instantly. There is a special promotion from January 1 to March 1, 2017. During this promotion the top prize is $20,000 towards trips, or $18,000 in cash. The statistics for this draw are currently based on taking a cash sum of $18,000. In addition to this, you also get an entry for the nightly lottery which has a top prize of $100,000. Unlike most lotteries, none of the prizes are shared, so you get the full $100,000 if there are multiple winners! Instead of using numbers the Wheel of Fortune lottery uses words. There is a pool of 39 words, 6 of which will be printed on your ticket. This is the exact same as if it was a lottery with balls numbered 1 through 39, except that words are used to fit with the overall theme.
