About Lottario

Lottario is an Ontario exclusive lottery that is drawn every Saturday night at 10:30PM EST. A single entry costs $1, each entry has 2 lines of 6 numbers. The minimum jackpot is $250,000, and each week the jackpot will grow until it is won. The jackpot is shared by all winners, however all other prize tiers are not shared, so getting 5/6 + the bonus will get you $10,000 regardless of how many other players receive that same prize.
