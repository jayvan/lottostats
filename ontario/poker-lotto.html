About Poker Lotto

Power Lotto is one of Ontario's instant win games, which give you a chance to win right away as well as a ticket to a lottery that night. For each $2 play you will be dealt 5 playing cards on the lottery terminal screen. Based on your hand you can win up to $5,000. You can choose to pay $1 more to go "All-In". It is almost never worth it to purchase All-In, you can see the details here. In addition to the instant win, you also get an entry for the nightly lottery which has a top prize of $100,000. In the nightly lottery 5 cards are drawn, the more cards you match, the more you win! Unlike most lotteries, none of the prizes are shared, so you get the full $100,000 if there are multiple winners!
