Encore Results, Statistics, Odds and Prizes

About Encore

Encore is an extra lottery you can play when you take part in OLGs other lotteries. Each entry costs $1, and you can win up to $1,000,000. For each of your entries you will receive a 7 digit number with each digit being 0-9. The more digits you match the better your prize! In general Encore doesn't have very good odds of winning, only 1 in every 9 entries will win a prize. To see Encore results visit the page for the lottery that you played Encore with.
