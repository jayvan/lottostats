About Megadice

Megadice is one of Ontario's instant win games, which give you a chance to win right away as well as a ticket to a lottery that night. For each $2 play you will see 7 dice roll on the lottery terminal. Based on your roll you can win up to $7,500. In addition to this, you also get an entry for the nightly lottery which has a top prize of $100,000. Unlike most lotteries, none of the prizes are shared, so you get the full $100,000 if there are multiple winners!
