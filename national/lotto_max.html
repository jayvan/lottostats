About Lotto Max

Lotto Max is a Canada wide lottery that is drawn every Friday night at 10:30PM EST. A single entry costs $5; each ticket has 3 lines of 7 numbers. The minimum jackpot is $10,000,000 and the maximum jackpot is $60,000,000. After the jackpot reaches $50,000,000 additional prize money is split into individual draws for $1,000,000 called MaxMillions. For example, the jackpot could be $60,000,000 and there could be 23 additional draws for $1,000,000; your ticket would be entered in the main draw, and 23 additional draws for more chances at a million dollars.
