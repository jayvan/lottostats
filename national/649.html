About Lotto 6/49

Lotto 6/49 is a Canada wide lottery that is drawn every Wednesday and Saturday night at 10:30PM EST. A single entry costs $3. The minimum jackpot is $5,000,000 and there is no limit to how large the jackpot can grow. The odds of winning Lotto 6/49 are significantly higher than Lotto Max, so the jackpot does not typically get as high. Each draw there is an additional guaranteed prize of $1,000,000 that is awarded to one of the tickets entered in the draw.
